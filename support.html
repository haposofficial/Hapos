Frequently Asked Questions

Is HaPOS really free?

Yes, 100% free. No subscriptions, no hidden charges, no ads. All features are available to everyone.

Does it work offline?

Yes. HaPOS is offline-first. All data is stored locally on your device. No internet is needed to use the app.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings → Backup. You can export a backup file and share it via WhatsApp or Email. Google Drive backup is coming soon.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Currently, HaPOS works on a single device. You can transfer data using the backup and restore feature. Multi-device sync is planned for a future update.

I forgot my PIN. What do I do?

PIN recovery is coming in a future update. For now, please contact us at hapos.official@gmail.com and we'll help you.

What businesses is HaPOS best for?

HaPOS is designed for distributors, wholesalers, small restaurants, cafes, and retail stores.

Can I print bills?

Yes. You can generate PDF invoices and print them or share via WhatsApp/Email. Bluetooth printer support is coming soon.
